Inspiration

Forever Falling

There were numerous sources of inspirations when thinking about this 2D web game. I could write a whole list of games and projects that helped me turn a difficult situation into something beautiful. However, the main source of this expression came from a dark reality, a time were I felt that I was falling from a high state of mind and seemed to last forever. Wishing there were magical abilities to slow everything down, just a little and gain a few seconds. Allowing the return of having control of the path ahead. Games like Portal helped me ground my feelings and turn them into an playable expression that I hope people will enjoy.
